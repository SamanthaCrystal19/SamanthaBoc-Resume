Samantha Boc

Film Maker

My name is Samantha Boc and I am currently a student at UCR. I am in the process of earning my Bachelor’s Degree in the Theater, Film, and Digital Productions Department. I have always been fond of creating and viewing films. My favorite genre of films is Horror, but I also enjoy a variety of other genres such as Action or Romance.

In my family, we respect as well as enjoy the entertainment industry. I have an older sister who has also graduated with a Film degree. We both enjoy analyzing the films like the angles used, the costumes, the storyline, and what message it is trying to convey.

Since I was raised in Hollywood, I had the privilege of visiting famous sights such as the Walk of Fame. I was fortunate to see many famous names and handprints of famous movie stars by the TCL Chinese Theater. I would also see popular events occurring such as The Oscars on my way to school. Being surrounded by the film industry helped me to discover my passion for film.
